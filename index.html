Food Around The Philppines

Region of Origin Food Description

Bicol

 Bicol Express

A dish cooked in coconut milk with fish, shrimp, or pork that is creamy and spicy and seasoned with chili peppers. It comes from Bicol and has a strong, deep heat.

Metro Manila

 Pancit Malabon

Thick rice noodles, shrimp sauce, and seafood combine to create a delicious noodle dish. It frequently has chicharon and boiled eggs on top.

Cebu

 Lechon Kawali

Deep-fried pork belly that is crispy and served with vinegar or liver sauce on the side for dipping. It's ideal for decadent eating because it's crispy on the outside and delicate on the inside.
